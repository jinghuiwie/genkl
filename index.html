About genKL

While the urban jungle further expands to form and create ever-increasing complexities, genKL is one of the iconic residential condominiums situated prominently in the Kuchai Lama urbanscape. The balance of hardscape and softscape is strictly implemented in this development with the abundance of greenery in the landscape and a naturally ventilated car park. Bringing out the best of modern lifestyles, genKL weaves seamlessly together with the existing greenery by creating a vast yet intricate piece of landscaping that highlights the best of the development.
